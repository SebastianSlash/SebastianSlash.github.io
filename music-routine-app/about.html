Music Routine App

Recently I've started to develop an app which is intended as a practicing tool for musicians. The Idea is to have an app where you create exercises, like scales, and also be able to incoperate them in practice routines.

Wheather you play the Piano, Guitar or Saxophone this app can be used for any instrument.

Currently the app is a very minimal working example showcasing the basic idea behind it.

Features

Create exercises you use to practice an instrument. Exercises can vary in complexity and are comprised of at least one interval.

An interval describes what you have to do for that part of the exercise, it can have a metronome and/or a timer.

When you practice your exercise you will see the interval desrciption a metronome indicator and timer indicator if applicable. After a exercise is done you will be asked for feedback to (in the future) adapt your exercise to your current level.

The app also includes a plain metronome that can be used without any exercises.
